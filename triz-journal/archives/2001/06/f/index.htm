(1) Course Material: CID Course for Children (3):
    1st Grade, 2nd Semester, "Fairy Tales School"
        (Guidebook & Workbook) Topics 1 to 5 (Finished)

by Natalia V. Rubina (Petrozavodsk),
English translation by Irina Dolina, Editing by Nakagawa

Children learn that the fairy tales are also composed of
components and are constructed in typical ways of relationship
in the story. Heroes of the fairy tales were faced with
difficulties and had to solve problems.

Children learn these with stories, games, puzzles, etc.
in a pleasant atmosphere of the class.
