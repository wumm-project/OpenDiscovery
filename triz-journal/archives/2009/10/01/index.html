    We're continuing our practice of publishing some of the best TRIZ conference papers - this month with a selection from TRIZCON2009.

    • Editorial panel member Jack Hipple looks at how to un-fuzzy the fuzzy front end of innovation with TRIZ.
    • Boris Zlotin and Alla Zusman present the objective existence of TRIZ solutions for problems emerging in different stages in Producing TRIZ Solutions: Odds of Success.
    • In Decision-making Model to Evaluate Highly-valued Designs, Manabu Sawaguchi explores a decision-making model to measure the ideality of products.
    • Applying TRIZ to Business Process Reengineering by Shree Phadnis and Aditya Bhalla looks at using problem flow technology as a way to identify a list of key problems that if solved improve business processes
