    By Jahau Lewis Chen

    The Second Annual Conference of the Taiwan TRIZ Association
    National Tsing Hua University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, December 15, 2007

     

    Scope

    The Taiwan TRIZ Association announces the call for papers for its second Annual Conference. The aim of this conference is to provide an opportunity for exchanging ideas among the people interested in and working with TRIZ in Taiwan. Additionally this conference provides the opportunity to network and promote the use of TRIZ in both industries and academia.

    Topics

    Suggested topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following.

    • TRIZ theory development: new methods, procedures and ideas
    • Application of TRIZ in all areas, particularly in patent research, eco-design, product design, Six Sigma, value engineering and machine design
    • Education and training of TRIZ: training courses in industries and universities
    • TRIZ practices in industry: case studies
    • Others: integration with other innovation tools

    Important Dates


    • August 15, 2007 - Submission of abstract (500 words)
    • September 15, 2007 - Notification of acceptance
    • October 15, 2007 - Submission of manuscripts
    • December 15, 2007 - Conference

    Conference Language

    The conference language is Chinese. However, English papers and presentations are also welcome.

    Submission of Abstracts

    All individuals who wish to present papers should submit an abstract (indicating the paper's title and the complete address of the author(s)) to the following email address: triz2007@chu.edu.tw.

    Introduction of Taiwan TRIZ Association

    The Taiwan TRIZ Association was established on July 23, 2005. Currently, it has more than 200 members and offers more than 10 TRIZ training courses on different TRIZ topics each year. The Taiwan TRIZ Association is planning to organize an Asia international TRIZ conference in the future.

    Report on the First Conference of Taiwan TRIZ Association

    National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan
    October 21, 2006 

     Figure 2: Attendees of the First Conference of the Taiwan TRIZ Association

    The First Conference of Taiwan TRIZ Association was held at National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan on October 21, 2006. This conference attracted more than 140 participants. A total of 41 papers were presented at the conference covering a variety of topics including:

    • TRIZ theory development
    • TRIZ industrial applications
    • Application of TRIZ in:

      • Patent research
      • Eco-design
      • System development
      • Product design
      • Machine design
      • Six Sigma & value engineering
